Here are some useful links to learn more about triathalons or research events that you can race in!

    IRONMAN.com

    IRONMAN. If you havnt heard of this extreme distance event you are in for a treat. The "crown jewel" of achievements for many triathlets.

    Triathlete.com

    Triathlete.com is the "go-to" for anyone seeking advice for triathalons. They have articles that range from nutrition, to injury prevention, to selecting the right gear. It is definately worth a look.

    Triathalon.org

    Triathalon.org is a website that tracks events in the triathalon world. If you take a quick look im sure you can find a race near you pretty soon!

    nytimes.com

    This article from The NY Times breaks down what triathalon is. It goes through the basics (some of which has been mentioned on this website), as well as how to train for certain distances. It also has a lot of other helpful information.

    Strava.com

    Strava is a social network for athletes. You can post your workouts and interact with other posts via "Kudos" and comments. You can also follow some of the best athletes in the world, given they have an account, and see the types of trainings they do.
